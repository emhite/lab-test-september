Shannon Poulsen

Graduate student


My research broadly concerns why people believe inaccurate information with a specific focus on cognition as a source of misinformation. For example, information that violates expectations is often misremembered. How can someone be informed if they don’t even remember accurate information? I use a variety of methods to test my claims, including eye-tracking experiments, longitudinal panel data, and online experiments. If you’d like to know more about my interests or published work, please visit my website at http://u.osu.edu/poulsen.6/
