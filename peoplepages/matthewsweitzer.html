Matthew Sweitzer

Graduate student


I am a Ph.D. candidate in communication at Ohio State. My research broadly encompasses political communication, social networks, and information processing. I specialize in computational approaches, such as dynamic networks, agent-based modeling, and both supervised and unsupervised topic modeling. In addition to my training at Ohio State, I have conducted research at the Santa Fe Institute as part of their Complex Systems Summer School, and I have also interned at Sandia National Laboratories. For more information about me, including an up-to-date CV, please visit my website: matthewsweitzer.com
